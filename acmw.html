ACM-W Core Team

The mission of ACM-W India is to carry on the work of the ACM community with a focus on empowering women in computing in India. The ACM-W provides a wide range of programmes and services to ACM members and works in the broader community to promote the contributions of technical women. ACM-W promotes, celebrates, and advocates on a global level for the full engagement of women in all elements of the computing industry. In order to draw new students into computer studies and keep them there, ACM-W Student Chapters are established. Professional Chapter-sponsored events give women information on computing vocations and chances to network.
